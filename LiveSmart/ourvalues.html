Values And Mission


Live Smart Healthcare's mission statement reflects our expanding role, as we focus even more strongly on prevention and wellness, and strive to improve the health of those who live in our communities.

Our Values


The Live Smart difference is the team approach, the commitment to excellence and the full-time dedication to rehabilitation. Rehabilitation is all we do. At Live Smart, every decision, resource and policy is designed to support the team.

With advanced tools and cutting-edge technology, we deliver the most advanced rehabilitation care available with compassion and enthusiasm. Our values are always focused on doing what’s right for each and every patient – delivering quality, cost-effective care with integrity and respect.


Our Mission


Our mission is to be the healthcare company of choice for patients, employees, physicians and shareholders by providing high quality care in the communities we serve. At Live Smart, the cornerstone of our operations is the delivery of quality healthcare in the most appropriate, safe, patient-centered environment.

Above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life. In recognition of this commitment, we strive to deliver high-quality, cost-effective healthcare in the communities we serve.

In pursuit of our mission, we believe the following value statements are essential and timeless:

  • We recognize and affirm the unique and intrinsic worth of each individual.
  • We treat all those we serve with compassion and kindness.
  • We trust our colleagues as valuable members of our healthcare team and pledge to treat one another with loyalty, respect, and dignity.
  • We act with absolute honesty, integrity, and fairness in the way we conduct our business and the way we live our lives.


Working well: our commitment to ethics and compliance


The Live Smart Ethics and Compliance Program helps ensure that everyone associated with Live Smart-affiliated facilities reflects the Mission and Values Statement, the Fundamental Commitment to Stakeholders, and other standards set forth in the Code of Conduct.

Taken together, these approaches to the operation of affiliated facilities set ambitious standards for how we treat our patients, the physicians who practice in affiliated facilities, one another and other groups with whom we interact.

If everyone associated with an HCA-affiliated facility reflects these standards in his or her activities, it will ensure that we provide compassionate, efficient, quality, patient-focused healthcare at all times.
